This is a branch with "work in progress" examples for ODMv2, especially on Workflows, developed by Jozef Aerts and other authors from the ODMv2 development team.
There is no claim at all that the examples are consistent and complete.

Each time a major decision about anything in these examples is taken by the ODMv2 development team,
we will try to update the examples as soon as possible (but without guarantee).
